Why Use This Tool?

  • 🔒 Enhanced Privacy on Shared Devices: Keep your bookmarks encrypted.
  • 📝 Secure Note Storage: Safely store encrypted, Markdown formatted notes directly in your bookmarks.
  • 🗂️ Consolidate Bookmarks: Combine multiple bookmarks into a single encrypted entry.
  • 📑 Temporary Notes in your open Tabs: Encrypt notes and leave them in a browser tab.
  • 🔗 Secure Bookmarking of Authentication Links: Encrypt URLs containing HTTP authentication credentials.
  • 🕵️‍♂️ Hide a bookmark from Autocomplete: Your browser will never reveal it in the address bar.
  • 🌐 Universal Browser Compatibility: Operates across all major browsers without special extensions.
